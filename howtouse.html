Prize Tracker

How to Use

Instructions
  1. Paste your PTCGO Deck Export into the Text Area and click Submit Decklist
  2. Once your deck loads to the right, click on the cards that you have prized
  3. Once you take a prize, click the prize card you have taken and it will be greyed out

Reset Prizes

  • To reset all the Prizes click the 'Return ALL Prizes' button
  • To reset the last prize you set (due to error or misclick) click the 'Return List Prize' button.
